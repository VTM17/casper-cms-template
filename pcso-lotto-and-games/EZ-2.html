PCSO EZ 2 Lotto


On July 2004, the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO) was introduced the PCSO EZ 2 Lotto a daily Digit game like Swertres Lotto.

PCSO EZ 2 Lotto was initially launched as an exclusive game for Luzon. However, due to the public’s request, In 2006, the EZ2 Lotto was opened to the Visayas-Mindanao players.

How to play PCSO EZ 2 Lotto?

Playing PCSO EZ 2 Lotto is easy. Just visit any authorized PCSO lotto outlet and get a PCSO EZ 2 Lotto playslip and mark 2 numbers (from numbers 1 to 31). You will pay ₱10 for every standard combination play.

You can also play the Rambolito.

To play this game, you must be 18 years old or above.

How does EZ-2 Rambolito works?

In rambolito, the system generates the permutation of the player’s chosen two numbers.

Example: If your chosen number combination is 12

You can win with these combination:
12
21
Giving you 2 chances to match the winning numbers.

How to win EZ 2 Lotto?

To win, you need to match correctly all the two (2) winning numbers in exact order.

How much can I win?

Standard play = ₱4,000
Rambolito = ₱2,000

Where can I see the EZ 2 Lotto results?

The draws is televise in PTV 4 at 9:00 pm. You can also find the official and verified EZ 2 Lotto results right here on the LottoInfo.ph website. Just head to the EZ 2 Lotto draw results page. The winning numbers are posted once the draw results have been verified and confirmed. However, we still encourage you to visit the official PCSO website if you still want to verify the game results there.

The draws are held three times a day, every 11:00 am, 4 pm and 9 pm.

How do I claim the money if I win?

Claiming your winnings is easy. If you win prizes amounting to ₱5,000 and below you can claim it at any authorized Lotto outlet or at the PCSO branch offices near you. If you win more than ₱5,000 to ₱20,000 you can claim it at any of the PCSO branch offices or at the PCSO head office and if you win more than ₱20,000, you should go directly to PCSO main office, located at 605 Conservatory Bldg., Shaw Blvrd. Corner Princeton St., Mandaluyong city. For more details about claiming your prize, click here.
