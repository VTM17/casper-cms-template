How to play PCSO Swertres Lotto

Lottery | May 21, 2020


PCSO Swertres

PCSO Suertres Lotto or commonly known as Swertres. Came from the two words, "Suerte" and "Tres" which means Lucky three. It is a daily digit game like EZ 2 lotto offered by the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO). The game was introduced on June 2002 and initially played only in Visayas and Mindanao. This is to address the increasing illegal numbers of game operations in the Visayas and Mindanao areas.

Due to insisting public demand, on September 2006 the PCSO Swertres Lotto game was made available in the Luzon area. PCSO Swertres Lotto is played three times daily, every 11am, 4pm and 9pm.

How to play Swertres Lotto?

Playing PCSO Swertres Lotto is simple. Go to any authorized PCSO lotto outlet and get your PCSO Swertres Lotto playslip and mark 3 digit numbers (from numbers 0 to 9) you can choose three same numbers (Example: 333). You will pay ₱12 for every standard Swertres combination play.

You can also play the Swertres Rambolito.

To play this game, you must be 18 years old or above.

How does Rambolito works?

In Swertres Rambolito, the system will automatically combine all the different possible combinations of your chosen 3 numbers. Giving you more number combinations.

Swertres Rambolito 6
In rambolito, If your chosen number combination is composed of three (3) different numbers, it will automatically fall under Rambolito 6.

Example: If your chosen number combination is 345

You can win with these combination:
345
354
453
435
534
543
Giving you 6 chances to match the winning numbers.

Swertres Rambolito 3
In rambolito, If your chosen number combination is composed of two of the same number and one different number, it will always fall under Rambolito 3.

Example: If your chosen numbers combination is 112

You can win with these combination:
112
121
211
Giving you 3 chances to match the winning numbers.

How to win Swertres?

To win Swertres, you need to match correctly all the three (3) winning numbers in exact order.

How much can I win?

Standard play = ₱4,500
Rambolito 3 = ₱1,500
Rambolito 6 = ₱750

Where can I see the Swertres Lotto results?

The Swertres draws is televise in PTV 4. You can also find the official and verified Swertres Lotto results right here on the PNKW.ph website. Just head to the Swertres Lotto draw results page. The winning numbers are posted once the draw results have been verified and confirmed. However, we still encourage you to visit the official PCSO website if you still want to verify the game results there.

The Swertres draws are held three times a day, every 11:00 am, 4 pm and 9 pm.

How do I claim the money if I win?

Claiming your Swertres winnings is easy. If you win prizes amounting to ₱5,000 and below you can claim it at any authorized Lotto outlet or at the PCSO branch offices near you. If you win more than ₱5,000 to ₱20,000 you can claim it at any of the PCSO branch offices or at the PCSO head office and if you win more than ₱20,000, you should go directly to PCSO main office, located at 605 Conservatory Bldg., Shaw Blvrd. Corner Princeton St., Mandaluyong city.
